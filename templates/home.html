Welcome to Dreasy, fast and easy tool to design your solar dryer

Schematic drawing of a ventilated tunnel solar dryer
(1) fans, (2) photovoltaic module, (3) transparent plastic sheet, (4) black matte floor, (5) tray with products to dry, (6) heating part and (7) drying part

The air enters the dryer through fans, supplied by a photovoltaic solar panel.
The air is heated up to the drying temperature in the heating section. The heated air enters the drying part and dries the product.
Dreasy helps you to calculate the airflow needed as well as the length of the heating and drying sections.
